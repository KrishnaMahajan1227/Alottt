Solarize ERP

Complete ERP solution for solar pump vendors supporting government initiatives

Client Solarize India
Year 2024
Services ERP Development, UI/UX

Overview

Solarize ERP is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning solution designed specifically for solar pump vendors operating within India's renewable energy initiatives.

The system streamlines operations for vendors participating in government schemes like PM-KUSUM (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha evam Utthaan Mahabhiyan) and Surya Ghar, providing end-to-end management from lead generation to installation and support.

Built with modern web technologies, the platform handles complex workflows including subsidy management, vendor onboarding, inventory tracking, and customer relationship management while maintaining compliance with government regulations.
